hello! i’m tatiana.

Experience Designer at Amazon Robotics

Originally from Canada, I studied architecture at Ryerson University in Toronto before journeying to Cambridge to work towards a Masters of Architecture. Along the way I had the opportunity to work at multiple companies, including Tesla in the Bay Area, PARTISANS in Toronto, and hold assistant positions within research and teaching where I investigated possibilities of extended realities, digital fabrication, and videogame architecture.

I continued to investigate my current interests in digital design, virtual spaces, computation, and futurology at Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Currently, I am doing an internship with the Experience design team at Amazon Robotics where I am working on prototyping augmented reality interfaces for fulfillment centers.
